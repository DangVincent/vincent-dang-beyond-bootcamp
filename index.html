instructions

  1. this game is played on a 3x3 grid where the player will face off against the computer by clicking on any cell of the grid to place your mark.
  2. the first player to either get three x’s or o’s in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.
  3. when all 9 squares are full, the game is a draw.
  4. have fun!
